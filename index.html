Platform

Design Principles

Long-Wing Solar Platform

A long wingspan with slightly curved, upward-angled wings maximizes sunlight exposure and glides smoothly for endurance missions.

Energy + Balance Core

A smooth, narrow central body houses batteries, AI processing, and communications to keep weight centered and stable during long flights.

Scientific Data Collection

Underside sensor modules are integrated near the nose and centerline to capture precise weather and environmental readings in-flight.
